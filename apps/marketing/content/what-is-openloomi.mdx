# What is OpenLoomi?

_Open-source AI coworker, driven by attention agent_

OpenLoomi is an open-source AI coworker. Centered on a desktop **attention agent**, it connects your authorized work tools and screen content, builds a **holistic context** of your people, projects, and decisions, and tells you what happened, why it matters, what to do next, and surfaces daily summaries — saving your attention for what matters.

## Deep Dive

### 🐾 Attention Agent — Your Always-On Desk Companion

It's 9 AM — you've just sat down at your desk, your inbox isn't even open yet, and the little fox (Loomi) in the bottom-right of your screen pops up a bubble: "Sarah's meeting invite still needs an RSVP — due by 11 AM today." That's the Attention Agent. It doesn't steal your focus; it just gently nudges you in the moments that matter.

- 🪟 **An always-on-top, transparent little fox** — 168×168, doesn't disappear when you close the main window, hides in the system tray and comes back with a single click.
- 🔔 **Only surfaces when it should** — an email that needs a reply, a meeting past its RSVP deadline, 9 AM rolling around — a single gentle nudge, nothing more.
- ✋ **You can interact with it directly** — click to open details, drag it anywhere on screen, right-click to pause, double-click to summon the main window.
- 🎭 **Sleep mask, wagging tail, little hops** — rich expression animations make it feel "alive" rather than a cold notification.

[Learn more →](/docs/attention-agent)

### 🧠 Holistic Context

OpenLoomi builds persistent knowledge graphs of people, projects, and decisions. Unlike other AIs that forget after each conversation, OpenLoomi's memory **grows on its own** — completely visible and auditable. Six months later, it still remembers your commitments and context. No more repeating yourself, no more context loss.

- Remembers Q3 partnership direction confirmed with Sarah 3 months ago (budget: $50K)
- Remembers demo feedback 6 weeks ago (needed security compliance materials)
- Today when Sarah emails, automatically pulls historical context and generates reply draft
- You can always see and audit exactly what OpenLoomi remembers about you

### 🔄 Loop — Scans Your To-Dos Every 10 Minutes

At the start of the day, what you really want to know isn't "how many unread emails do I have", but "what do I actually have to personally handle today". That's exactly what Loop is for. Every 10 minutes it picks out the signals scattered across your email, calendar, PRs, and @mentions, prioritizes them, and turns them into "decision cards" placed in front of you — you glance at the preview, agree, tap "Approve", and Loop acts on your behalf.

- 📥 **See** — pulls signals from email, calendar, GitHub, Slack, and Linear that are relevant to you
- 🧠 **Understand** — uses OpenLoomi memory to add context to each signal (who the sender is, which project, what you last discussed)
- 🪪 **Decide** — tags each signal with a type (`email_reply` / `rsvp` / `review_pr` / `im_reply` / `todo`) and a confidence score
- 🛡️ **Protect** — Dry-run always comes first, **execution always waits for your Approve**

[Learn more →](/docs/loop)

## A Complete Intelligence Loop: From Perception to Action

OpenLoomi implements a full **"Receive → Process → Remember → Understand → Serve"** loop — mirroring how a capable human colleague operates:

| Layer             | What It Does                                                                                                                       |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **📡 Receive**    | Multi-platform, multi-modal ingestion — IM, email, documents, files, web data, voice calls — unified into one pipeline             |
| **⚙️ Process**    | Noise reduction at scale — deduplication, OCR, ASR, intent extraction, semantic clustering — turning chaos into structured signals |
| **🧠 Remember**   | Persistent knowledge graph — people, projects, decisions — survives conversations, platforms, and months of time                   |
| **🎯 Understand** | Deep semantic intent, not just keywords — cross-modal understanding, emotional tone, contextual relevance                          |
| **🚀 Serve**      | Proactive delivery — smart summaries, auto-replies, scheduled reports, personalized alerts — without being asked                   |

### Memory That Compounds Over Time

Inside OpenLoomi, memory is layered into multiple levels of abstraction:

- **Raw information** — original messages, files, transcripts
- **Information insights** — extracted entities, decisions, key events, timeline, event execute diff including why, what and how
- **Contextual memory** — recent conversation state, temporary context
- **Knowledge-base memory** — long-term people/projects/preferences knowledge graph

This layered memory enables OpenLoomi to reason across both immediate context and deep historical knowledge simultaneously.
A message from a client today is understood in light of a negotiation three months ago — automatically.

OpenLoomi also exposes clean APIs through skills for all features. Critically, it provides APIs to control its own plane — meaning
OpenLoomi can invoke itself to complete tasks, enabling self-iteration and continuous improvement without external prompting.
