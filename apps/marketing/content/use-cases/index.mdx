# Use Cases

OpenLoomi empowers professionals across industries to automate repetitive workflows through natural conversation. Each use case below is a worked example — the inputs, the connectors it touches, and the typical output you'd see.

## Overview

The use cases are grouped into three flavours:

- **Proactive & always-on** — workflows where OpenLoomi watches your day and surfaces decisions before you ask. Backed by [Loop](/docs/loop).
- **Content & research** — workflows that turn raw feeds (X, RSS, industry news) into briefings and engagement drafts. Backed by [Connectors](/docs/connectors) + [Scheduled Jobs](/docs/automation).
- **Team workflows** — async standups, daily syncs, and relationship-management loops for distributed teams. Backed by [Memory](/docs/memory) + [Tasks](/docs/automation).

Pick the use case that matches your day-to-day, or skim the headlines below for one that sparks an idea.
